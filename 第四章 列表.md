## 4.1 创建List
注意``[]``和``[[]]``的区别，也可以用``$``来选定
```r
> j<-list(name="joe",salary=55000,union=T)
> summary(j)
       Length Class  Mode     
name   1      -none- character
salary 1      -none- numeric  
union  1      -none- logical  
> j$name
[1] "joe"
> j[1]
$name
[1] "joe"

> j[[1]]
[1] "joe"
> j
$name
[1] "joe"

$salary
[1] 55000

$union
[1] TRUE
```

也可以通过vector来创建  
```r
> z<-vector(mode="list")
> z[["abc"]]<-3
> z
$abc
[1] 3
```
